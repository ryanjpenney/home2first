Ambassador Program

In late 2022, to expand Home2First's reach across Arizona and the Dominican Republic, we launched our Ambassador Program. It enlists high school student-athletes to champion the mission at their own schools. They run equipment drives, mentor younger players, and help bring the game to the kids who need it most.
