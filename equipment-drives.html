Equipment Drives

Collecting new and gently used gear across Arizona — and shipping it to youth academies in the Dominican Republic.

JANUARY 24, 2026 · 330+ PIECES COLLECTED

Horizon High School — 2nd Annual Drive

Nearly double last year's total — over 330 pieces of equipment including ~30 bats, ~15 gloves, and 150+ baseball pants and jerseys. Led by Ambassadors Ethan Trahan and Maverick Bolen, with top donations from the Schue, McKendry, and Weber families.

NOVEMBER 16, 2025 · 400+ PIECES COLLECTED

Saguaro High School — 4th Annual Drive

Smashed the previous year's total with over 400 pieces of gear — 64 pairs of pants, 42 pairs of cleats, and 33 bats. Led by Ambassadors Davis Diller and Conner Hansen, with top donations from the Dravecky, Heskett, and Caminiti families — including cleats from Atlanta Braves first-rounder and former Saguaro star Cam Caminiti.

AUGUST 2025 · 175+ PIECES + $10,600 RAISED

Cold Beers & Cheeseburgers — 4th Annual Drive

Our longest-running partnership keeps growing: more than 175 pieces of equipment (including 20+ bats and 60 gloves) collected across all 17 Valley locations, plus a $5,300 donation that Cold Beers matched — bringing the total contribution to $10,600 in support of the BHBL in East Phoenix.

DECEMBER 2023 · RECORD 1,100+ PIECES

Valley Christian High School — Record Drive

Our single largest equipment drive ever: a record-setting 1,100+ pieces of donated equipment, led by Ambassador Caleb Sweeney. The majority traveled to the Dominican Republic with Valley Christian's school trip the following March.

NOVEMBER 13, 2023 · 440+ PIECES COLLECTED

Chaparral High School — 2nd Annual Drive

Timed to the end of the D-backs' World Series run, the Firebird family delivered one of our largest single-day drives ever — about 440 pieces, a 10% increase over the first year. Top donors: the Kilber, Epting, and Newstrom families.

NOVEMBER 12, 2022 · 400+ PIECES COLLECTED

Chaparral High School — The First School Drive

Our first-ever high school equipment drive set the template for everything since: over 400 pieces donated in two hours at the Chaparral baseball field. Top donors Reagan Widdows, Liu Rodríguez, and Devin Long each took home a Cold Beers & Cheeseburgers gift card.
