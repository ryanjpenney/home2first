Equipment Drives

Every season, Arizona high schools rally to collect new and gently used gear — fueling the Balsz Home2First League at home and our humanitarian shipments to the Dominican Republic. Scroll through the drives, most recent to oldest.

FEBRUARY 15, 2026

Perry High School — Inaugural Drive

Over ~125 items including ~20 high-quality bats, ~15 gloves, and several full sets of catcher's gear. Led by Ambassador Richie Cruz.

JANUARY 24, 2026

Horizon High School — 2nd Annual Drive

Over ~330 pieces — nearly double the prior year's ~175 — including ~30 bats, ~15 gloves, and 150+ pants and jerseys. Top donors: the Schue, McKendry, and Weber families.

DECEMBER 13, 2025

Chaparral High School — 4th Annual Drive

Over ~320 pieces including ~35 high-quality bats — about a 10% increase over the prior year. Led by Ambassadors Max Mangini and Keone Ede, with top donations from the Mangini, Burggraf, and Ede families.

NOVEMBER 16, 2025

Saguaro High School — 4th Annual Drive

Over 400 pieces — smashing the prior year's ~275 total — including 64 pairs of pants, 42 pairs of cleats, and 33 bats. Top donors: the Dravecky, Heskett, and Caminiti families, including cleats from Cam Caminiti.

NOVEMBER 2, 2024

Saguaro High School — 3rd Annual Drive

Over ~275 pieces. Led by Ambassadors Cam Caminiti, Davis Diller, and Conner Hansen, with top donations from the Chavez and Johnson families.

OCTOBER 19, 2024

Horizon High School — Inaugural Drive

Over ~175 high-quality pieces. Led by Ambassador Ethan Trahan and Coach Beau Polvorosa, with top donations from the Beyer and Weber families.

SEPTEMBER 2024

Chaparral High School — 3rd Annual Drive

Over ~300 pieces — including our first-ever pitching machine. Led by Ambassador Brady Short, with top donations from the King and Shuba families.

JANUARY 20, 2024

Saguaro High School — 2nd Annual Drive

~165 pieces including 46 high-quality bats. Led by Ambassadors Cam Caminiti and Davis Diller.

DECEMBER 13, 2023

Valley Christian High School — Inaugural Drive

Record-setting ~1,100 pieces — our single largest equipment drive in 3+ years. Led by Ambassador Caleb Sweeney, with the majority bound for the Dominican Republic via Valley Christian's March school trip.

NOVEMBER 2023

Chaparral High School — 2nd Annual Drive

Over ~440 pieces — roughly a 10% increase year-over-year and one of our largest single-day drives ever. Top donors: the Kilbers, Eptings, and Newstroms.

NOVEMBER 2022

Saguaro High School — Inaugural Drive

Over 200 high-quality items — kicked off with new Ambassador Cam Caminiti driving the effort.

NOVEMBER 12, 2022

Chaparral High School — Inaugural Drive

Over 400 pieces collected — one of the biggest drives to date at that point. Top donors: Reagan Widdows, Liu Rodríguez, and Devin Long.
