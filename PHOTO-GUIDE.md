# Photo Guide — where every photo goes

Each folder in `photos/` is one section of the website. Open the README.txt inside
for the exact filenames. Use .jpg (or .png for logos). The site checks for these
files automatically — drop a photo in, refresh the page, and it appears.
If a file is missing, the labeled blue placeholder keeps showing instead.

## photos/01 - Mission Hero
The big photo next to 'Our Mission' on the About and Our Mission pages.
- `mission-hero.jpg` — Wide/landscape shot (~4:3, at least 1120px wide) that captures the mission — kids playing, gear handout, or a team huddle.
