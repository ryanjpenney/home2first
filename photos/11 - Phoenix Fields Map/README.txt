Photos for the East Phoenix satellite fields map (the "Our home fields in East Phoenix"
section on the homepage, and the standalone fields-map.html). Each photo appears inside
that field's map-pin popup.

Drop your photos in this folder using EXACTLY these filenames (.jpg as listed).
As soon as a correctly-named file is here, it automatically appears on the website.

  promise-field-brunson-lee.jpg  ->  Popup photo for Promise Field at Brunson-Lee (landscape ~2:1, at least 600px wide).
  griffith-elementary-field.jpg  ->  Popup photo for Griffith Elementary Field (landscape ~2:1, at least 600px wide).
