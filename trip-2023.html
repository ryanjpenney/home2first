HUMANITARIAN TRIPS / THIRD ANNUAL TRIP

JULY 29, 2023

Meeting High Demands

Delivering 550 lbs of high-demand essentials to player-heavy regions.

By our third summer, the word was out, and the requests kept coming. The 2023 trip answered with 550 pounds of high-demand essentials delivered to the regions where the most players needed them.

The shipment (roughly 50% more equipment than the year before) was fueled by a banner collection year in Arizona: 400+ pieces from our first Chaparral High School drive, 200+ quality pieces from the Saguaro drive led by our first brand ambassador Cam Caminiti, and 125+ pieces from our new partners at Between the Lines, who also donated over 150 pairs of socks for the trip. We even packed 50 pairs of sunglasses, the most coveted item from the year before. Some equipment also stayed home to aid the Tonalea middle school baseball team.

Meeting high demand takes a whole community: ambassadors, sponsors, families, and the players who pass their gear forward.
