Three ways we get kids on the field

Your generosity directly fuels our expanding programs — powering our fast-growing East Phoenix youth league, shipping thousands of pounds of gear to the Dominican Republic, and removing financial barriers for Arizona athletes.

BHBL — Balsz Home2First Baseball League
